About Us

ALgo42 is a student-led AI/ML club dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and learning in artificial intelligence and machine learning. We believe in hands-on projects, insightful events, and sharing knowledge to shape the future.

Join us to collaborate with like-minded peers, participate in hackathons, attend talks by AI leaders, and build cutting-edge projects.
